We Are CHARIS-IMPACT

At Charis-Impact Church, we are a community driven by the grace and power of God to make a lasting impact in the world. Rooted in the love of Christ, we are committed to sharing His message of hope, healing, and transformation. We believe in empowering individuals to walk in their God-given purpose, grow in faith, and serve with compassion. Through worship, fellowship, and outreach, we strive to create a space where lives are changed, and God's Kingdom is advanced. Our mission is to reflect the grace (Charis) and power (Impact) of God in everything we do, reaching hearts and communities with the love of Christ.

Our Purpose.

The purpose of the church is to glorify God, foster spiritual growth, and serve as a community of faith for worship, discipleship, and fellowship. It exists to proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ, nurture believers in their spiritual journey, and demonstrate God's love through acts of service and compassion.

Our Mission.

The mission of the church is to fulfill the Great Commission by making disciples of all nations (Matthew 28:19-20) and to embody the Great Commandment by loving God with all our hearts and loving others as ourselves (Matthew 22:37-39).

For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life. For God did not send his Son into the world to condemn the world, but in order that the world might be saved through him.

John 3:16-17 ESV

Our Values.

Identity in Christ

Our church values faith in God, love and compassion, worship and prayer, biblical teaching, community and fellowship, spiritual growth, evangelism, integrity, unity in diversity, and the transformative power of hope in the gospel.

Church Is Family

The church is a family where believers come together as brothers and sisters in Christ, united by faith and love. It is a place of belonging, where we care for one another, support each other in times of need, and celebrate life’s blessings together. As a family, we grow spiritually through worship, prayer, and the study of God’s Word, encouraging one another to walk in faith and fulfill God’s purpose. The church is also a safe haven where everyone is valued, embraced, and nurtured, reflecting the unconditional love of God while serving and impacting the world around us

Kingdom Culture

Our Kingdom culture as a church is centered on worshiping God, loving unconditionally, walking in faith, pursuing holiness, embracing unity in diversity, serving with humility, spreading the gospel, living generously, transforming lives, and building God’s Kingdom on earth.

Spirit Led Life

Our Spirit-led life is marked by surrender to the Holy Spirit, allowing Him to guide, empower, and transform us daily. We seek His presence through prayer, worship, and obedience, walking in faith, wisdom, and discernment. Through the Spirit, we bear the fruit of love, joy, peace, and righteousness, living as vessels of God’s purpose and reflecting Christ in all we do.
