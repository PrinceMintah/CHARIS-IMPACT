Welcome to CHARIS-IMPACT

At Charis-Impact Church, we are a community driven by the grace and power of God to make a lasting impact in the world. Rooted in the love of Christ, we are committed to sharing His message of hope, healing, and transformation. We believe in empowering individuals to walk in their God-given purpose, grow in faith, and serve with compassion. Through worship, fellowship, and outreach, we strive to create a space where lives are changed, and God's Kingdom is advanced. Our mission is to reflect the grace (Charis) and power (Impact) of God in everything we do, reaching hearts and communities with the love of Christ.
